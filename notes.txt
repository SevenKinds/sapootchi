LOYALTY PET ("Sapootchi") — DESIGN NOTES
=========================================
Living braindump of every idea, major and minor. Not a spec — this is where
loose ideas and locked decisions both live. Mark items: [LOCKED] decided,
[TODO] needs a number/decision, [IDEA] maybe-later.

NORTH STAR
----------
- It's a daily-habit engine for a brand loyalty program, disguised as a pet.
- Every session must answer: "How did my pet CHANGE because I came back today?"
  If the only answer is "a bar went down", we failed.
- Session target: under 2 minutes.
- Design test for any feature: "does this make someone more loyal to the brand?"
  — NOT "should this give points?"

CORE LOOP
---------
- Open -> pet reacts to seeing you (mood greeting) -> care 30-90s -> one
  mini-game -> earn coins/food -> spend on cosmetics/decor OR watch it evolve
  -> leave.
- [LOCKED] Guarantee at least ONE surprise/change per session (gift, event, or
  needy moment) — not just maintenance. This is what delivers the north star.

CURRENCIES (two, strictly separated)
------------------------------------
- Coins (soft): from care, mini-games, perfect-care bonus, achievements.
  Spent on cosmetics, food, decor. No fraud risk -> client-side in POC.
- Brand Points (hard): from real brand actions + rare care milestones.
  Real-world value. Scarce, capped, server-authoritative. OUT OF SCOPE for POC.
- [IDEA] mini-game Result can pay Items OR Coins — interface must not assume one
  currency per game (catch-food pays food items, others pay coins).

STATS — visible (four meters)
-----------------------------
- Happiness (fun mini-game, petting, friendships, events)
- Hunger (feeding food items from inventory)
- Hygiene (scrub mini-game / bath)
- Energy (INVERTED — see below)
- [LOCKED] Decay = function of elapsed WALL-CLOCK time, not frames/sessions.
- [LOCKED] Hunger decays 25%/day -> full->empty in 4 days. One standard food
  = +25% = exactly one day of hunger; 4 items empty->full.
- [TODO] Decay rates for Happiness / Hygiene (start gentle, similar order to
  hunger; tune so a daily player stays comfortable).
- [LOCKED] ENERGY IS INVERTED (unlike the other three):
    * RISES passively while idle ("doing nothing") and faster by resting/sleeping.
    * SPENT by activity (mini-games — the runner especially).
    * At 100% -> ENERGIZED: hyper; the only way down is a mini-game (idle just
      refills it). A needy "let's play!" state.
    * At 0% -> FALLS ASLEEP on the spot; can't be interacted with; sleeps
      (fast regen) until Energy reaches 50%, then wakes. Hysteresis: sleep
      trigger 0, wake threshold 50.
- [TODO] Tune energy rates: idle regen, sleep regen, mini-game energy cost.
- [LOCKED] SOFT-FAIL: if Hunger hits 0 -> the pet RUNS AWAY.
- [LOCKED] Runs-away recovery: you get a prompt to LEAVE FOOD OUT to attract him
  back. Each day after (while food is left out) there is a 28% chance he returns.
  Recoverable, never permanent death.
- [TODO] Neglect cascade idea: while a stat sits at 0, Happiness decays faster.
- [TODO] Offline decay clamp (cap applied decay ~48h) so long absences aren't
  hopeless — revisit now that hunger runs-away at 0.

STATS — hidden (drive evolution + flavor, never shown)
------------------------------------------------------
- Intelligence (memory-type games)
- Friendship (social features)
- Personality (see below)
- Age (wall-clock)

PERSONALITY & MOOD (cheap depth)
--------------------------------
- Goal: players say "my pet is a menace", not "my pet is level 4".
- Personalities (same mechanics, diff anims + dialogue):
    Lazy (sleeps more, dislikes exercise)
    Hyper (bounces, energy drains faster, loves runner)
    Greedy (excited by coins, begs for snacks)
    Curious (finds random items, wants to explore)
- [LOCKED-for-POC] Personality ASSIGNED at hatch (random). Emergent personality
  is a bigger v2 system — keep it out of v1.
- Mood (separate from stats, derived from stats + recent events): Happy,
  Hungry, Excited, Bored, Sleepy, Curious, Lonely. Mood changes ONLY animations
  + dialogue, no gameplay effect.
- [TODO] Mood precedence rule (e.g. Hungry overrides Bored overrides idle-Happy).
- Idle animations — pet never stands still, cycle every 10-30s: sleeping,
  chasing tail, dancing, stretching, looking at you, falling over, yawning,
  eating crumbs.

FOOD & INVENTORY
----------------
- [LOCKED] Feeding mini-game rewards FOOD ITEMS (to inventory), not direct fill.
- [LOCKED] Catch-food mini-game awards 1-3 food items based on score.
- [LOCKED] Standard food = +25% hunger (4 -> empty to full).
- Food variety: Apple +25% | Sandwich +50% | Cake +10% hunger & +happiness.
- Inventory enables hoarding, choice, premium brand-food later.

MINI-GAMES (each one IS a care action)
--------------------------------------
- Catch falling food -> feeding -> Hunger (via items). [SHIP IN POC]
- Fun game (Simon/memory) -> playing -> Happiness (+hidden Intelligence).
- Scrub-the-pet (timing; simple action ok for POC) -> bath -> Hygiene.
- Dodge/runner (Subway-Surfers-style) -> exercise -> Energy.
- Petting (tap/stroke, always available, no game) -> small Happiness.
- [LOCKED] Generic interface: Play() -> Result{Score, Coins, StatDelta, Items}
  so each game is a plug-in. Ship catch-food only in POC.

SOCIAL / FRIENDSHIP (boosts Happiness) — phased
-----------------------------------------------
- Visits (POC-friendly, faked/local): visit another pet, wave, gift ->
  +happiness both.
- Best Friends: pets randomly meet; after several days become BFFs, appear in
  each other's homes.
- Co-op mini-games (highest engagement, latest phase).
- [IDEA] For POC without server, simulate friends with NPC pets so the hook
  exists day one.

DAILY & RETENTION
-----------------
- Perfect Care bonus: all four stats full at least once/day -> pet hugs you
  ("Perfect Care!") -> +10 coins. Emotional moment first, coins second.
- Daily gift: pet brings something small each day (pebble, flower, hat, leaf,
  mystery egg).
- Decay-while-away + ONE gentle notification: "your pet misses you".
- Streak flame: visible daily-care streak, escalating coin bonus.
- Daily quests (1-2): "play 1 mini-game, feed 3 times" — structure for aimless.
- Random events (~15%/day): tiny choice, tiny reward, big personality
  (butterfly: catch or watch fly? / scared: comfort or ignore?).
- Needy moments (the tamagotchi steal): pet NEEDS you, not just empty bars —
  wants to show a drawing, wants a bedtime story, learned a trick wants
  applause, scared of thunder. No reward needed every time.

ACHIEVEMENTS -> COSMETICS (not coins; visible rewards motivate more)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- Fed 100 times -> Golden Spoon
- 30-day streak -> Halo
- Runner 500x -> Running Shoes
- Perfect care x50 -> Golden Crown

COIN SINKS
----------
- Pet cosmetics: hats, accessories, skins.
- Home decoration (long-term sink): wallpaper, floor, bed, plant, TV, window,

- Seasonal items (cheap FOMO, not predatory): Halloween pumpkin hat, Santa hat,

- [TODO] Coin economy numbers: faucet ~30-60 coins/day engaged; prices — cheap

EVOLUTION (top retention lever)
-------------------------------
- Branching, driven by hidden stats + care patterns (NOT linear).
        Egg -> Baby Blob -> {Cute -> Royal | Smart -> Wizard | Wild -> Ninja}
- Overfeed -> chubby form. Lots of runner -> athletic. High happiness -> cheerful.
- Slow reveal over days; players replay to see other branches.
- Cheap to build: state + sprite swaps.
- [LOCKED] Egg phase is near-instant: start -> get an egg -> hatches straight
  away. First real phase is already the BABY.
- [TODO] Evolution triggers/thresholds (baby -> branch: on which hidden stat is
  highest at what age).

WORLD FLAVOR (cheap, later)
---------------------------
- Fake weather (rain/snow/sun/wind): background changes, pet comments.
- Seasonal events: reskins + one limited cosmetic per event.

LOYALTY INTEGRATION (post-POC) — frame every brand action as CARE
-----------------------------------------------------------------
- Scan receipt -> premium food item
- Buy featured product -> exclusive toy
- Visit a location -> travel sticker
- Refer a friend -> pet gains a companion

ART / SPRITES
-------------
- [LOCKED] assets/blob.png = THE BLOB, full/adult size (green, big googly eyes).
- [LOCKED] Baby = the same blob sprite rendered at 60% scale of the full size.
- For POC, fake art hard: shape/emoji sprites + squash-stretch tweens carry more
  life than expected and cost nothing. The "feels alive" animation/mood system
  is the real engineering cost (~70% of effort, 100% of the magic), NOT the sim.

ARCHITECTURE (Go / Ebiten)
--------------------------
- Scene manager: Scene interface (Update, Draw) + a stack — Home, MiniGame,
  Shop, DressUp. Ebiten imposes no structure; decide day one.
- Simulation package with ZERO Ebiten imports. Pet state = plain data; decay =
  pure function of elapsed wall-clock time; render = pure view of state.
  Single most valuable boundary: unit-testable, server-portable (hard currency
  later), renderer-agnostic.
- Mini-game plug-in interface (see MINI-GAMES).
- POC state entirely local (coins only, no brand points). Note: local clock =
  local cheat (device clock manipulation breaks streaks/decay) — acceptable POC,
  a reason streaks/hard-points move server-side later.

POC CUT LINE (recommended v1)
-----------------------------
IN:  one pet; 4 visible stats + wall-clock decay + runs-away-at-0; Home scene
     with feed/bathe/rest + petting; ONE mini-game (catch-food); inventory +
     food items; coins + a small shop; DressUp scene; mood->animation (placeholder
     shapes ok); one needy moment; local save; instant egg->baby + ONE evolution
     step (branching stubbed).
OUT: social/NPC visits, achievements, home decor, daily quests, weather,
     seasonal, brand points, full evolution tree.
Proves the only two things a POC must: is the care-loop warm, and does earning
coins feel good.
